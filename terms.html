5. Payment Terms

5.1 Platform Fees

Minerva charges a commission of 20-30% on completed lessons, depending on teacher tier and subject. Specific commission rates are clearly displayed on the platform during registration and may be updated with 30 days notice. Teachers set their own rates, which are displayed on the platform.

5.2 Escrow System

Payments are held in escrow until the lesson is confirmed complete. Refunds are processed according to our refund policy:

  • Lessons cancelled 24+ hours in advance: Full refund
  • Lessons cancelled within 24 hours: 50% refund
  • No-show by teacher: Full refund to student
  • No-show by student: No refund, payment to teacher

5.3 Commission

Teachers agree to pay Minerva a commission on all completed lessons. Commission rates are clearly displayed and agreed upon at registration. First 100 registered teachers receive zero commission for their first four classes.

5.4 Payment Release

Funds are released within 3 business days of lesson completion confirmation by both parties. During peak periods, release may take up to 5 business days.
