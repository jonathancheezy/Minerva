Project Minerva Co-Founder Pitch Deck

The Minerva Effect: Unlock Your Potential

Why Minerva?

Named after: The Roman goddess of wisdom, learning, and strategic insight.

Our belief: Education is not just about information it's about transformation.

The Minerva Effect: We connect students with verified teachers who unlock their full potential backed by trust infrastructure no other platform in Asia offers.

The Problem

For Parents:

  • Trust No way to verify if kids are safe around strangers
  • Quality Unable to assess teacher prior to engagement and ongoing
  • Prepayment Risk Teachers can disappear with money, no performance rating
  • Scheduling Coordinating multiple tutors = chaos, no central view
  • Progress No systematic way to track learning outcomes

For Teachers:

  • Fair Pay Agencies take 40-60% commission for just making an introduction
  • Student Flow No platform to get discovered, dependent on word-of-mouth
  • No-Show Risk No protection from last-minute cancellations
  • Scheduling Managing dozens of parents via WhatsApp, double-booking
  • Branding Unable to build personal brand and track record online

Current reality:

  • No platform solves trust + escrow + progress tracking in HK
  • Word-of-mouth is the default unsafe and inconvenient
  • Teachers pay 40-60% to agencies just for introductions

Our Solution

Project Minerva The AI-powered learning marketplace that puts trust first.

What we do:

  • Two-sided marketplace: students ↔ verified teachers
  • Subjects: Academic, Music, Language, Arts, Sports + AI-learning
  • Insurance-backed, escrow-protected, progress-tracked

Competitive advantage:

  • Liability coverage (no HK competitor has this)
  • Full background verification (ID + refs + certs)
  • Escrow payment protection money held until lesson complete
  • AI-powered progress dashboard for parents
  • First lesson free sponsored by Minerva

Geographic rollout:

  • Stage 1: Hong Kong (MVP launch Q3 2026)
  • Stage 2: China & India
  • Stage 3: Singapore & Asia
